Who can apply

You can apply if:

  • your farm is in England
  • you grow, or intend to grow, irrigated crops for the food industry
  • you own the land the project will be on or have a lease for at least 5 years
  • the estimated project costs are over £87,500

What the grant is for

The Farming Transformation Fund water grant is to make more efficient use of water for irrigation and secure water supplies for crops.

Grants can cover up to 40% of the eligible costs of a project.

The minimum grant you can claim is £35,000 (40% of £87,500). The maximum grant is £1 million.

The grant can pay up to 40% of the costs for:

  • constructing water storage reservoirs
  • abstraction points, pumps and pipework to fill a reservoir
  • irrigation pumps, controls, underground water distribution systems
  • water meters
  • best practice application equipment such as boom or trickle irrigators
  • software and sensors to optimise water application

The business must have a water meter or plan to install one as part of the project.

How to apply for a grant

Check if your project can apply for a grant

You need to check if you’re eligible and how well your project fits the funding priorities.

You need:

  • information about your business (number of employees, turnover etc)
  • information about the project (irrigated land in hectares etc)
  • a list of the items you’d like to buy for the project
  • an estimate of the total cost of the items
  • to either have or be confident you will have any planning permission and abstraction licences before 21 October 2021

After you have gone through the checking service/checker, you’ll get a score showing how well your project fits the funding priorities.

Depending on how well your project scores, you may want to submit your details to RPA to get the full application.

Submit a full application

Once you submit a full application, RPA will assess how well it is.

You will not automatically get a grant. The grant is highly competitive and you are competing against other projects.

If your full application is successful, you’ll be sent a funding agreement and can begin work on the project.

When to apply

You can check if you’re eligible and how well your project fits the funding priorities from 21 May 2021 to 20 July 2021.

You can submit a full application from 21July 2021 to 21 October 2021.

Your project must be completed before you can claim the funding. All claims for funding must be made before 21 March 2022.

If your project needs planning permission or an abstraction licence, you may want to have these already.

Starting the project or committing to any costs (such as placing orders) before you receive a funding agreement will invalidate your application.

Before you start the project, you can:

  • get quotes from suppliers
  • apply for planning permission or an abstraction licence
