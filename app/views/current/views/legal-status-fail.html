You're not eligible for large grants

The large grants scheme is only open to businesses which have one of the following legal statuses:

  • Sole Trader
  • Partnership
  • Limited Company
  • Trust
  • Limited Liability Partnership
  • Charity
  • Community Interest Organisation
  • Public Organisation

Other types of business may be supported in future schemes. See the large grants guidance for further information.
